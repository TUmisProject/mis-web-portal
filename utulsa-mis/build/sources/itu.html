Information Technologists United Overview

Information Technologists United, also known as iTU, is a professional organization for MIS majors and minors. Through peer to peer mentoring, professional development events, and company presentations, iTU prepares undergraduate students for internships and jobs relating to MIS.

Membership Requirements

  • Pay dues of either $20 per year or $15 per semester
  • Maintain a minimum GPA of 2.5
  • Attend at least 3 iTU sponsored events per semester
  • Are you an iTU Member in Good Standing?
