iTU Officers

Name Position Email Description
Abby Dolan President abby-dolan@utulsa.edu

The iTU president is responsible for overseeing the iTU program as a whole, including coordinating monthly Drofessional Development meetings, planning mentorship events and banquets, and serving as the liaison between MIS students, MIS faculty, and IT professionals wishing to come meet TU MIS students. The President makes sure each officer is fulfilling their respective duties and serves as a resource if any additional assistance is required for operations and logistics within the organization to run smoothly.

Rachael Gantner Vice President rachael-gantner@utulsa.edu

The iTU Vice President is responsible for assisting the President in coordinating monthly Profesional Development meetings and ordering food for executive meetings for the officers. Their primary responsibility is planning the iTU End of the Year Banquet where MIS seniors and outstanding TU MIS Alumni are recognized and installations for incoming iTU officers take place.

David Rohfing Treasurer david-rohlfing@utulsa.edu

The iTU Treasurer is responsible for handling all finances for our organization. They ensure our budget is kept on track, dues are collected from members, and reimbursements are taken care of properly and in a timely manner.

Melanie Cummings Secretary melanie-cummings@utulsa.edu

The iTU Secretary is responsible for recording officer meeting minutes and ensuring iTU members are properly accounted for attending the monthly Professional Development meetings. They also create the digital signage to be displayed in various places throughout campus, as well as the iTU information sent to all MIS majors and minors prior to our monthly Professional Development meetings.

Gunner Jensen Web Master gunner-jensen@utulsa.edu

The iTU Web Master is responsible for maintaining the MIS website, iTU social media, and overseeing all technology-related tasks.

Jocelyn Sheffield Senior Representative jocelyn-sheffield@utulsa.edu

The Senior Representative is responsible for communication with the Junior class about all things iTU. This involves encouraging attendance, getting people excited about events, and continuing to spread iTU throughout campus. Like the Junior Representative, recruitment and communication is their primary job.

Ryan Token Junior Representative ryan-token@utulsa.edu

Junior Representative is responsible for communication with the Junior class about all things iTU. This involves encouraging attendance, getting people excited about events, and continuing to spread iTU throughout campus. Recruitment and communication is their primary job.

Ashley Etter Mentorship Chair ashley-etter@utulsa.edu

The iTU Mentorship Chair is responsible for advertising and arranging iTU Mentorship events, pairing MIS students interested in the iTU mentor program with MIS mentors, and general recruitment of Management Information Systems majors and minors for iTU.
