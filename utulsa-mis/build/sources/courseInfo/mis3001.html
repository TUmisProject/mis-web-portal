Management Information Systems - MIS-3001


Course Number: MIS-3001
Title: Advanced Spreadsheet Applications
Synonym: 82180
Course Description: Students will use various advanced functions of spreadsheets that will allow them to make more efficient and effective business decisions. Students will further develop skills and gain knowledge through the use of hands-on exercises to be completed outside of class.
